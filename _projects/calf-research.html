Calf Research

Our research focuses on calf health, particularly body weight prediction and detecting signs of diarrhea using advanced AI techniques. We employ methods such as YOLOv8 for object detection and threshold-based segmentation methods to improve prediction accuracy.

Key Features

  • Object detection with YOLOv8
  • Body weight prediction using depth images
  • Threshold-based segmentation for improved accuracy
