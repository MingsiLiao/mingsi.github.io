Research Projects

My research combines computer vision, machine learning, multimodal AI, and bioinformatics to address challenges in animal health, precision livestock farming, and biological data analysis.

AI & Computer Vision

Computer Vision-Based Diarrhea Identification

Automated diarrhea identification in pre-weaning dairy calves using rear-end images collected from automatic milk feeders.

Computer Vision · Deep Learning · LVLM Benchmarking

Automated Calf Weight Estimation from Depth Images

Automated body weight prediction using depth cameras, contour extraction, sensor data, and machine learning.

Depth Imaging · Machine Learning · Sensor Integration

Multimodal Weaning Age Prediction

Integrating feeding behavior, growth records, health monitoring, computer vision, microbiome profiles, and environmental data for precision weaning management.

Multimodal AI · Time-Series Analysis · Precision Livestock Farming

Early Disease Forecasting

Multimodal AI models integrating behavioral, physiological, imaging, and health records to predict disease risk before clinical symptoms appear.

Health Monitoring · Multimodal AI · Forecasting


Genomics & Bioinformatics

Early Lactose Exposure and Calf Development

Longitudinal investigation of early-life lactose supplementation, calf growth, health, and microbiome development.

16S rRNA · Microbiome · Longitudinal Analysis

Rumen Metatranscriptomics in Dairy Cows

Functional profiling of rumen microbial ecosystems using large-scale metatranscriptomic sequencing and pathway analysis.

Metatranscriptomics · Functional Genomics · Microbiome

Genetic Association Analysis in Ovine Johne's Disease

Genome-wide association analysis to identify genetic variants associated with Johne's disease susceptibility in sheep.

GWAS · SNP Analysis · Statistical Genomics

Sheep Breed Differentiation Using Whole-Genome Sequencing

Whole-genome sequencing analysis of genetic variation, population structure, and breed differentiation in sheep.

WGS · Population Genomics · Variant Analysis
